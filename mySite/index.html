About Adam Revet

Adam Revet has been making videos in some form since the third grade. His love for both directing and acting in short films has flourished into something he is now pursuing as a career.

Hobbies

Although his hobbies are rooted deeply within the tech and multimedia realms, at an even earlier age Adam became interested in sports like soccer and basketball, subsequently going on to find his skill for volleyball was something to pursue. From this interest grew a passion, landing him a starting position on the Lethbridge College Kodiaks as a middle blocker.

Origins

Adam comes from humble beginnings growing up in North Battleford, Saskatchewan. Surrounded by a large family of 5 siblings, his love and appreciation for close-knitted systems and dynamics has molded him into a natural leader. His leadership shines through in all that he does, whether it be in the form of directing a video or helping his teammates on the court.
