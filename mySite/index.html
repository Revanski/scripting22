About Adam Revet

Adam Revet has been making videos in some form since the third grade. His love for both directing and acting in short films has flourished into something he is now pursuing as a career.

Hobbies

Although his hobbies are rooted deeply within the tech and multimedia realms, at an even earlier age Adam became interested in sports like soccer and basketball, subsequently going on to find his skill for volleyball was something to pursue. From his interest grew a passion, landing a starting position on the Lethbridge College Kodiaks as a middle blocker.
