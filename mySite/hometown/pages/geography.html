Golden is situated directly on the cusp of the Columbia Rockies, neighbouring the East and West Kootenay Mountain ranges. Like most of the southeastern part of British Columbia, Golden is dominated by countless mountains, rivers, streams, and lakes, all of which harbour thriving wildlife in some capacity. The feeling of being surrounded by such lush diversity in geography is incredibly grounding. The powerful mountains towering above the residents of Golden can be intimidating to some but are humbling to most.

Another hour or more eastward is the metropolis of Calgary, making the location of Golden far enough to be secluded but close enough for the occasional trip into the city.
