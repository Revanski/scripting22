Golden, British Columbia is home to a considerable variety of people despite its population being on the lower side – bordering 4,000 people in recent years.

Demographics show that Golden’s residents are a little on the older side when compared to the rest of Canada, although its average age is about 3 years below the province’s average. Its older age may help explain its above-average household income numbers, which is further explained later. Golden is far from the most diverse in terms of its visible minority population, but due to it being a required stop for many passing through to other parts of BC, the residents here are known for their hospitality and kindness to all passersby and new residents regardless of their ethnicity. On top of this, Golden has numerous places of worship that cater to the spiritual needs of several different religions. Golden is considered by many to be a very laid-back and warm town, and much of this warmth can likely be attributed to the behaviour of its locals, with stats to back it up: AreaVibes states that Golden’s violent crime rate is 18% lower than the national average.
